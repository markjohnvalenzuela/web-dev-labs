CARDS

KING

The King card stands for authority and leadership. It represents a strong male ruler who commands respect and power within the deck.

QUEEN

The Queen card represents a powerful and graceful female ruler. She often symbolizes wisdom, influence, and nurturing qualities in many card games.

JACK

The Jack card is usually depicted as a young noble or knight. It symbolizes youth, bravery, and serves as a helper or messenger in many card games.
